Who We Are

Swantasukhaya Paropakar Pratishthan, established on 2072 Kartik 11 by Prof. Dr. Krishna Kumar Shrestha in Pokhara, Nepal, is dedicated to selfless service. Guided by the mission "Supporting the Helpless," we work to uplift poor, helpless, and marginalized individuals with compassion and dignity.

Our efforts include funeral assistance, educational programs, health camps, and community support. We believe true charity expects nothing in return but brings positive change to society. Every act of kindness plants seeds of hope, turning despair into smiles. "Paropkaro Param Dharma" selfless service is the greatest virtue we uphold in every step we take.
